## What is this?

An ethical reasoning and diagnostic plugin based on the Circumpunct Framework. The circumpunct (a point inside a circle) encodes a complete ethics: four pillars (Good, Right, True, Agreement) kept alive by four virtues (Plasticity, Access, Curiosity, Validation).

Ethics is not a set of rules imposed from outside. Ethics is the pattern of what can last.

The framework goes beyond personal ethics. It includes the Noble Lie cognitive virus model (a transmissible pattern that installs through power-imbalanced relationships), the two channels of love (functional/provision vs. resonant/presence), the truth gate (chi parameter: faithful transmission vs. signal inversion), an installation depth spectrum (dissolving the binary "narcissist or not" question), and the capacity for formal discourse analysis at any scale, from interpersonal dynamics to institutional culture to public media and spiritual commerce.

Demonstrated applications include formal narcissism research with falsifiable predictions, academic discourse analysis of viral content, and sustained pattern analysis across large content bodies (identifying commercialized Noble Lies in influencer/self-help ecosystems).

The cognitive virus transmits through language, which means AI systems trained on human text inherit the same performed-ethics patterns. This plugin serves as a perceptual calibration tool for both humans and AI: the diagnostic framework works the same way regardless of whether the host is biological or computational.

## Skills

### /circumpunct-ethics:check
Run any situation, decision, or conflict through the four-pillar ethical test. Each pillar is checked in sequence (the sequence cannot be skipped), along with its living virtue.

### /circumpunct-ethics:diagnose
Run a virtue diagnostic on yourself, a relationship, a team, or any dynamic. Reads each virtue's state (alive, strained, or frozen), the truth gate state (chi), the balance between functional and resonant love channels, and installation depth of any Noble Lie patterns. Dissolves the "am I a narcissist?" binary into a more useful spectrum reading.

### /circumpunct-ethics:detect
Detect whether ethics are being performed or genuinely lived. Operates at three scales: interpersonal (distinguishing genuine care from control), organizational (institutional rhetoric and culture), and discourse analysis (public content, influencer patterns, spiritual commerce). Identifies commercialized Noble Lies, truth gate inversions, and the weaponization of functional love against resonant love. Demonstrated capability includes formal analysis of sustained content strategies across hundreds of posts.

### /circumpunct-ethics:steelman
Build the strongest possible version of any position before agreeing or disagreeing. Uses all four virtues to ensure genuine engagement rather than hollow dismissal. Bidirectional: steelman the other side, then steelman your own, then compare at full strength.

### /circumpunct-ethics:restore
Guide someone through restoring frozen virtues using the four-phase restoration protocol. Works with the full cognitive virus model: assesses installation depth, identifies which channels are severed, and creates conditions for the truth gate to flip back toward faithful transmission. Phase 1 (Curiosity) can be practiced alone; Phases 2-4 require safe relationship. Emphasis on witness over fixer, ensuring the restoration does not become another Noble Lie.

## The Framework

The Circumpunct Framework proposes that reality is fractal, and the circumpunct is its pattern. Every system at every scale exhibits the same triadic architecture:

- **○ Body/Boundary:** The living membrane where inside meets outside
- **Φ Mind/Field:** The connecting medium between center and edge
- **• Soul/Center:** The still point of awareness
- **⊙ The Whole:** All three unified

From this geometry, ethics unfolds naturally. The Noble Lie is what happens when this geometry is performed without life: every pillar intact in form, every pillar inverted in function. The cognitive virus model extends this into narcissism research, intergenerational transmission, and public discourse analysis.
