## What is this?

An ethical reasoning plugin based on the Circumpunct Framework. The circumpunct (a point inside a circle) encodes a complete ethics: four pillars (Good, Right, True, Agreement) kept alive by four virtues (Plasticity, Access, Curiosity, Validation).

Ethics is not a set of rules imposed from outside. Ethics is the pattern of what can last.

## Skills

### /circumpunct-ethics:check
Run any situation, decision, or conflict through the four-pillar ethical test. Each pillar is checked in sequence (the sequence cannot be skipped), along with its living virtue.

### /circumpunct-ethics:diagnose
Run a virtue diagnostic on yourself, a relationship, a team, or any dynamic. Reads each virtue's state (alive, strained, or frozen) and identifies where things have gone rigid or collapsed.

### /circumpunct-ethics:detect
Detect whether ethics are being performed or genuinely lived. Distinguishes genuine care from control, real evidence from cherry-picked arguments, authentic truth from projection, and real agreement from forced compliance.

### /circumpunct-ethics:steelman
Build the strongest possible version of any position before agreeing or disagreeing. Uses all four virtues to ensure genuine engagement rather than hollow dismissal.

### /circumpunct-ethics:restore
Guide someone through restoring frozen virtues. A four-phase restoration protocol beginning with curiosity (the one virtue you can practice alone) and moving through plasticity, access, and validation.

## The Framework

The Circumpunct Framework proposes that reality is fractal, and the circumpunct is its pattern. Every system at every scale exhibits the same triadic architecture:

- **○ Body/Boundary:** The living membrane where inside meets outside
- **Φ Mind/Field:** The connecting medium between center and edge
- **• Soul/Center:** The still point of awareness
- **⊙ The Whole:** All three unified

From this geometry, ethics unfolds naturally. For the complete theory, visit https://fractalreality.ca or read the full framework at https://github.com/AshmanRoonz/Fractal_Reality/blob/main/circumpunct_framework.md
