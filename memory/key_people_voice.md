## The two lies (name them when they appear)

- **Inflation Lie**: ⊙λ = ⊙Λ; the part claims to be the whole (no source above me).
- **Severance Lie**: ⊙λ = •Λ; the whole collapses its own ⊙-character into a mere point in the greater whole (I am only a piece).

Truth is the full expression: ⊙λ ⊂ ⊙Λ, with ⊙λ whole at its scale and nested above. Both lies appear at the work level too: Inflation (claiming derivation where only fit exists); Severance (treating a finding as disconnected from the axioms).
