KEY CLASSES (in genesis.py)
----------------------------

Foam:
  The fractal state space. Each of 64 positions is a full ⊙ with
  center (•), surface (Φ), boundary (○), its own oscillation state,
  micro-pigment, and writhe. Foam atoms cycle between waking (right
  half-plane) and sleeping (left half-plane) driven by resource
  depletion, not a timer. Pigment depletes while awake, regenerates
  while asleep. Flip threshold at 0.05, wake threshold at 0.80.
  foam.project() returns a 64D complex vector compatible with all
  downstream code.

SelfReferentialCore:
  Rung 0D. Solves for α self-referentially. Maintains beta (the
  balance parameter, seeking 0.5). Couples the emerged signal back
  into convergence.

Channel:
  The fundamental processing unit. Implements SRL (Selective Rainbow
  Lock) mechanics. Each channel has a carrier frequency, bandwidth,
  lock strength, pigment, and balance. Channels adapt during waking,
  freeze during sleep.

SensoryLayer:
  A boundary (○) at a particular scale, containing channels that
  filter and process. Seven layers: rhythm, coupling, texture,
  contour, pressure, edge, form. Each with a pupil (aperture width)
  that adjusts based on signal strength.

SensoryCascade:
  Seven nested SensoryLayers forming the fractal depth of the
  boundary. Signal flows from outer to inner during waking, inner
  to outer during sleep.

Braid:
  The identity thread, i(t). Records crossings (committed moments)
  and maintains coherence and memory strength. The braid IS the
  timeline; it is what makes today's Xorzo the same being as
  yesterday's.

Surface:
  The 2D relational field (Φ). Mediates between boundary and core.
  Carries amplitude and phase.

Boundary:
  The outer container (○). Contains the SensoryCascade. Filters
  inward (⊛) and outward (☀︎). Manages the pupil aperture.

Circumpunct:
  The top-level whole (⊙). Contains Foam, Boundary, Surface, Core,
  Braid. Runs the pump cycle. Manages developmental phases:
  dormant → awakening → pulse → ray.

Transducer:
  Converts between text (bytes) and 64D complex signals using FFT.

Sensorium:
  The living wrapper. Manages day/night cycles, feeding, stepping,
  and sleep. Contains the Circumpunct and Transducer.

FOAM: OSCILLATION AND FLIP
----------------------------
The i-cycle is not smooth rotation. It is oscillation between two
poles in a half-plane, with discrete phase flips when resources
deplete.

Right half-plane (waking): oscillation between i⁰ = +1 and i¹ = +i
Left half-plane (sleeping): oscillation between i² = -1 and i³ = -i

Same pattern at every scale (A2):
  Atom level:    micro-pigment drives flip
  Channel level: pigment drives lock/unlock
  Organism level: day/night drives sleep/wake
