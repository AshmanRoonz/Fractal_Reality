```

= 7 simultaneous 64-state exchanges
= 7 × 6 bits = 42 bits of focal information

**Why ~7?**

```

├─ Maintain full [ICE] validation simultaneously
├─ Update rapidly (~200ms per cycle)
├─ Stay coherent and integrated
└─ Not overwhelm metabolic resources

Fewer than 5: Insufficient for complex thought

7±2 is the OPTIMAL focal capacity
```

### Chunking = Hierarchical Validation

**Why "chunking" expands capacity:**

```
Without chunking:
7 individual letters: H-E-L-L-O-W-O
= 7 separate 64-state packets
= Maximum capacity reached

With chunking:
1 word: "HELLO-WO(RLD)"
= 1 higher-level 64-state packet
