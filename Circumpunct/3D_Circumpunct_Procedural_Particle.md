# The 3D Circumpunct: Spacetime's Fundamental Procedural-Particle

## Abstract

The circumpunct ⊙ is not merely a symbol—it is a 3-dimensional structure comprising aperture (•, 0D), boundary (○, 2D), and field (Φ, 3D). This 3D completeness is the mechanism of scale recursion: every completed ⊙ₙ becomes the aperture •ₙ₊₁ of the next octave. There is no privileged scale, no dimensional ceiling. The trinity is not *in* the recursion—the trinity *is* the recursion.

The circumpunct encodes three distinct dimensional objects:

```
⊙ = • ⊗ ○ ⊗ Φ

  •  = aperture    (0D point)
  ○  = boundary    (2D surface)
```

This is not metaphor. The circumpunct *is* 3-dimensional:
- The center is dimensionless

The span from 0D to 3D = **3 dimensions**. This is the step size.
