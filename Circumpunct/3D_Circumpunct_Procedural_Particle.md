```

> The aperture field is i, copied and rotated in different ways, at every point in spacetime.

```

⊰  :  emergence      (center → field)

4. It **emerges** outward again (⊰): z' is re-expanded into a new field a'(x,t).

In words:

> The world gathers into the soul,
> the soul transforms it by a pure perpendicular turn,
> then radiates it back out as an updated world.

This is the "breath" of the circumpunct — the fundamental in–through–out process.
