## Research Areas

This project spans several interconnected domains:

**The Circumpunct Framework** is the core theoretical work: a single geometric structure (⊙) that formally derives quantum mechanics, general relativity, the Standard Model gauge group, and fundamental constants from first principles; all with zero free parameters.

**Consciousness Research** applies the framework to model consciousness as cross-scale phase-locking of aperture cycles. The Theory of Mind (Ch. 19) treats the psyche as source (•), surface (Φ), and boundary (○), with mental health understood as range of motion along that axis rather than a fixed state.

**Narcissism and Pathology Research** uses the framework's ethics architecture to formalize how lies install, how apertures close, and how cognitive viruses propagate. Includes diagnostic tools, restoration protocols, and the four-virtue model (Plasticity, Access, Curiosity, Validation).

**AI Research** explores the collaboration between human and AI in theoretical development. Claude (Anthropic) has been a co-author and development partner throughout; the framework itself was refined through sustained human-AI dialogue.

**The Circumpunct Ethics Plugin** is a Claude skill suite built on the framework's ethics architecture. It includes five tools: `/check` (four-pillar ethical test), `/detect` (performed vs lived ethics; cognitive virus detection), `/diagnose` (virtue state diagnostic), `/restore` (frozen virtue restoration protocol), and `/steelman` (bidirectional strongest-version argument building). These are practical applications of the framework's ethics chapters, packaged for real-time use.
