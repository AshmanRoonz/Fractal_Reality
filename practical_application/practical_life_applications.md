## 2. The [ICE] Decision Framework

### Every Choice Has Three Checks

When facing any decision, ask:

**[I] Interface - Boundaries**
- Does this maintain healthy boundaries?
- Can I engage without losing myself?
- Will this respect others' boundaries too?
- **Good** = Integrity of boundaries

**[C] Center - Coherence**  
- Does this align with who I actually am?
- Is this authentic to my values?
- Does this create internal coherence or conflict?
- **True** = Authentic identity

**[E] Evidence - Reality**
- Is this grounded in actual reality?
- Am I working with facts or fantasies?
- Will this have real consequences?
- **Right** = Alignment with what's real

**If all three pass → The choice validates**  
**If any fail → The pattern will dissolve (suffering follows)**

### Example: Job Offer

**[I]:** "Do the work hours respect my boundaries? Can I maintain relationships outside work?"  
**[C]:** "Does this work align with my actual values, or am I just chasing money/status?"  
**[E]:** "Is the salary real? Is the company stable? Am I being realistic about the role?"

**All three pass → Take it**  
**Any fail → Don't take it (no matter how tempting)**

- Stop consuming, start producing

**Signs you're β < 0.5 (too much emergence):**
- Scattered, overwhelmed
- Too many projects, can't focus
- Manic energy, burnout approaching
- "I just need to do more"

**Fix:** More convergence
- Rest, integrate
- Read, learn, receive
- Let things come TO you
- Stop pushing, start allowing

### Daily Practice

**Morning:** Check your β
- Too heavy/stuck? → Plan emergence (create, act, express)
- Too scattered/chaotic? → Plan convergence (rest, read, integrate)

**Goal:** Keep returning to balance
