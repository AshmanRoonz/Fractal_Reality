Every entity faces both directions.

There is no thing that is only a part. There is no thing that is only a whole. The atom is not "between" its electrons and its molecule — it is a whole containing them and a part contained by it. Simultaneously.

This is ⊙.

Boundary faces up toward what it's inside of.
Field is the relation between them.

One structure. Every scale. Both directions at once.
