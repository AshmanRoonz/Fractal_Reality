⊙ Chakras

Same soul. Different frequencies. Felt in different locations.

The chakras are frequency bands of a single aperture — not separate apertures.
One soul (•), seven songs.

The Seven Frequency Bands

Chakra • Aperture Φ Field ○ Boundary

The heart sits at the midpoint. Below: existence, desire, will (self-oriented). Above: truth, insight, unity (transcendence-oriented). The heart is where self-concern transforms into other-concern — where the aperture balances.

Cascade Principle

Blockage at any level distorts everything below it.

A blocked heart means will operates without love — that's domination.
A blocked throat means loving will cannot speak its truth — that's silent resentment.
A blocked third eye means expression without clarity — that's noise.

Healing often proceeds root upward: establish ground, then feeling, then power, then love, then truth, then sight, then opening.
