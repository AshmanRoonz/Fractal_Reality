The Dimensions of Experience

The dimensional ladder is not a model of experience; it IS experience. Eight stations, from pure attention to recursion. Integer dimensions are what experience settles into. Half-integer dimensions, carried by i, are experience happening.

Structural (integer): what things ARE
Processual (half-integer): what energy is DOING

The completed becomes the new beginning. Closed boundary becomes new aperture. What you learned is now the lens through which you see next. Identity arrived at (not assumed): the product of full rotation. 3.5D = 0D at the next octave. Experience does not end; it nests.

i rotates through all four processual dimensions. That rotation IS experiencing. The integer dimensions are what experience crystallizes into; the half-integer dimensions are experience itself: the gathering, the turning, the unfolding, the closing-that-becomes-opening. At every scale (a single thought, a conversation, a life, the cosmos) the same octave plays.
