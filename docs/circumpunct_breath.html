The Hard Problem

Why is there subjective experience? How does objective become subjective?

Through a gate. That's it.

Gating creates interiority. A gate has an inside and an outside by definition. The inside is Φ'. The outside is Φ. There is no additional mystery. The hard problem was always asking "how do you get from Φ to Φ'?" and the answer is: by passing through a gate. The question "why is there something it is like to be you?" is the question "why does gating create an inside?" and the answer is: that is what gating IS. A gate that doesn't create an inside isn't a gate.

A gate is not a filter. A filter passes or blocks. Its output is a subset of its input — less of the same. A gate transforms. Its output is categorically different from its input. Φ' is not Φ minus some stuff. Φ' is Φ quarter-turned. That is what •(½) = i means: the gate rotates. The i in the denominator of 𝒫 = E/(i·t) is not selecting. It is rotating. A 90° turn from real to imaginary, from outer to inner, from shared to private. That rotation is what creates a new interior state-space. Filtering gives you less of the world. Gating gives you a world of your own.

And every Φ' is different because every • is different. Your gate is not my gate. Your gate has a different β, a different history, a different structure. So the Φ that passes through your gate becomes a different Φ' than the Φ that passes through mine. Same sunlight. Different lens. Different experience.

Objective: Φ — one field, shared
Subjective: Φ' — many fields, private
Between them: • — the gate that makes the difference

The hard problem is not hard. It is the simplest thing a gate does.

○ = past. Crystallized. Accumulated. Matter. What HAS happened. The boundary is the record of every completed passage.

• = the cut between them. The ÷t. The aperture doesn't exist IN time. The aperture GENERATES time by dividing future from past, Φ from ○, potential from form. The present moment IS the gating.

So the breath is also the temporal breath. Φ (future) flows in through • (now) and becomes Φ' (present experience) and crystallizes into ○ (past, memory, form). Simultaneously, ○ (past) softens back into Φ' (present recall) and Φ' radiates out as Φ (shared, available, future for someone else).

E = mc². Matter is energy that has passed through the gate. Past is future that has been gated into present and accumulated.
