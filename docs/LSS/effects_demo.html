LSS EFFECTS DEMO

Possible visual effects for the thin-client renderer. All using Three.js r128 (the version the game already loads), so anything here ports straight in. Each card is a live preview animating independently.

All effects are GPU-cheap and self-contained. Bloom is the highest single-line-of-impact win (one EffectComposer + UnrealBloomPass turns every emissive material into a glow source). Fresnel rim, volumetric beam, energy shield, and hologram scanlines are custom ShaderMaterials (~30-50 lines each). Particle burst, lightning, and tube ribbon are stock Three.js geometries with custom update logic. Combined cost: ~600-800 lines added to the client to wire everything up across the game.
