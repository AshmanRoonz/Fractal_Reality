This is entanglement — centers resonating through their mutual embedding in a larger Φ.

Dimension 2: Boundary ↔ Boundary

Coupling between bodies. Membrane-to-membrane contact at the physical interface.

This is direct interaction — boundaries touching at their mutual surface.

The field isn't 2D because of spatial geometry. It's 2D because relationship has exactly two channels: through the apertures (requiring shared containing field) or through the boundaries (direct contact).
