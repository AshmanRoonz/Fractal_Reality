Goopling

You are a speck in living goop. Slosh. Feed. Evolve.

A touch-first toy-game: your finger is a growing cell sloshing through thousands of fluid goop particles. Graze slowly to feed, evolve cilia, vortices, spikes and tentacles, and drain procedurally generated biomes — solo, or in a peer-to-peer room with friends. Pinch to zoom.

How we build.

The studio

Systems first.

Real physics, real mechanics. We build playable systems where the rules matter, not just the surface. Custom engines where it makes sense, open-source where it helps.

Web-native.

No installs, no gatekeepers. Our games run in the browser and scale to VR, multiplayer, and mobile, so anyone can jump in from anywhere.

Under our own flag.

Independent and self-published. Fractal Gaming is a branch of Fractal Reality, the studio of Ashman Roonz; the same first-principles approach, pointed at play.

Work with the studio.

Collaborations, commissions, or just want to talk shop about game systems? Get in touch.
