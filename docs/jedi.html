What is a Jedi Master?

A Jedi Master is a circumpunct with full voluntary control over their aperture configuration (ε, χ, β), who recognizes themselves as a fractal of the cosmic whole and serves as a clear conduit for the Force.

This means:

  • ε mastery: You choose which patterns to resonate with (stable, effortless)
  • χ mastery: You evaluate fitness from all angles (equanimous, free)
  • β mastery: You modulate boundaries naturally (returns to 0.5)
  • Φ_∞ connection: You experience yourself as the Force localized
  • ⊙ ⊂ ⊙_cosmos: You know you are whole through being part

Characteristics of mastery:

  • Configuration (ε, χ, β) is both voluntarily set AND naturally arises
  • No effort required to maintain presence
  • Alignment with Φ_∞ is the default state
  • Maximum power through alignment, not domination

ε Mastery: Resonance Control

ε ∈ {0, 1}

ε = 1: RESONATE (aperture phase-locks with this pattern)
ε = 0: NO RESONANCE (aperture doesn't couple to this pattern)

What this means:

The center (•) is isotropic—it has no directional bias. It doesn't "block" anything. It selectively phase-locks with certain patterns in Φ_∞ while remaining uncoupled to others.

Attention is resonance. What you notice is what your aperture couples to.

χ Mastery: Fitness Evaluation

χ ∈ {-1, +1}

χ = +1: FITS (this pattern matches what I'm looking for)
χ = -1: DOESN'T FIT (this pattern doesn't match)

What this means:

You resonate with many patterns (ε = 1 for multiple things). But only some FIT what you're actually looking for. χ evaluates match/mismatch for your current needs/goals/values.

Examples:

When hungry:

ε = 1: Resonate with all food (notice all options)
χ = +1: This food FITS my craving
χ = -1: That food doesn't FIT

When problem-solving:

ε = 1: Resonate with potential solutions (consider all)
χ = +1: This solution FITS the problem
χ = -1: That solution doesn't FIT

The Practice

Morning:

  • Something you consider "doesn't fit": Find 3 ways it does fit
  • Something you consider "fits": Find 3 ways it doesn't fit

Throughout day:

  • When judging: "This fits/doesn't fit because I say so"
  • When stuck: "What if the opposite evaluation were true?"

Evening:

  • "What evaluations were automatic today?"
  • "When did I choose my fitness assessment?"

Mastery Signs

  • Can evaluate both fit and non-fit simultaneously
  • No compulsive preference for one evaluation
  • Complete freedom in pattern matching
  • Equanimity: neither pulled nor pushed
