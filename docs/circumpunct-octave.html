The operator engine · 4th roots of 1

Tap an operator (left) or a root (above).
The four processes are the fourth roots of unity. Imaginary axis = in/out (convergence & emergence, conjugates). Real axis = split/return (branching & recursion). Moving two rungs multiplies by i² = −1: every operator’s antipode is its own negation.

Read the ladder two ways

Legend

Structures (integers) are dimensions: 0D, 1D, 2D, 3D.
Operators (halves) are not dimensions: they act between or within structures.
Why the halves are not “dimensions.” Only 1.5 earns a measured fraction (a coastline’s Hausdorff dimension, ~1.25). For 0.5, 2.5, 3.5 the half is ordinal (it sits between structure n and n+1), not metric. One word was doing two jobs.
The convergence wrinkle. Convergence is many-to-one, inward: it builds the point. So its meaning fits the ∞ → 0D gap, not the 0D → 1D slot it occupies. Either a half-rung is missing above 0D, or it is mislabeled. Flagged, left in place.
Still owed. The vertical gap from 0D to ∞ asserts that the center is not the greater whole. That inequality is exactly what non-dualism denies. The ladder draws the debt; it does not yet pay it.
